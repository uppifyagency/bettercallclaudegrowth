# Glossary — The SUCKS Framework (Kieran Drew)

**1/3rd Rule** — Before publishing, delete a third of your draft to raise the word:wisdom ratio (Ch 8).

**80:20 of copywriting** — The SUCKS framework is the ~20% of skills driving ~80% of results; "the rest is just reps" (Ch 1).

**Authority by Association** — Borrowing credibility from trusted third parties (studies, stats, big-name endorsements); the easiest level of proof (Ch 4).

**Breadcrumbs** — Small personal details dropped consistently so readers get to know you and become fans (Ch 3).

**Clarity** — Making smart things easy to understand rather than sounding smart; the first "C" (Ch 5).

**Conversational** — Writing as if speaking with a familiar person, not at an audience; the third "C" (Ch 7).

**Curiosity** — Creating the urge to keep reading via four triggers; the second "C" (Ch 6).

**Customer Results** — Proof of outcomes you've created for others; the strongest level of proof (Ch 4).

**Distillation** — Turning complexity into simplicity; the writer's real job (Ch 5).

**Grade 4 (Hemingway)** — Readability ceiling: never publish above grade 4 reading level (Ch 8).

**How To's → How I's** — Rewriting generic advice from personal perspective to build relatability (Ch 3).

**Kept Simple** — Explaining things the best, not the shallowest; the "K" (Ch 8).

**Niche down** — Narrowing your topic so content reliably hits what your audience wants (Ch 6).

**One True Fan** — The single ideal reader you write each piece for (Ch 2).

**Open loop** — An unresolved promise/question that pulls the reader forward until closed (Ch 6, demoed in Ch 7).

**Persuasion Proof Stack** — Three escalating proof levels: Authority by Association → Your Results → Customer Results (Ch 4).

**Quantify** — Using specific, non-round numbers to create authority and belief (Ch 2).

**Rule of One** — Per piece, pick One Fan, One problem, One solution, One story (Ch 2).

**Slippery Slope** — Structure that pulls the eye downward so readers finish the piece (Ch 9).

**Specificity** — Precise, concrete writing that paints a clear mental picture; the first "S" (Ch 2).

**SUCKS Framework** — Pre-publish checklist: Specific · Unique/Useful/Undeniable · Clear/Curious/Conversational · Kept simple · Structured (Ch 1).

**Structured** — Skimmable formatting + varied rhythm; the final "S" (Ch 9).

**Surprise Document** — A running file of interesting things you save to fuel the "didn't-know" curiosity trigger (Ch 6).

**Undeniable** — Backing claims with proof; answers "Why should I believe you?" (Ch 4).

**Unique Mechanism** — A branded/named framework that makes an old idea new and un-copyable (copywriting term) (Ch 3).

**Useful** — Solving real reader pain and signaling the payoff clearly (Ch 3).

**Voice / Style** — Your natural way of speaking transferred to the page; developed by writing to a familiar person (Ch 7).

**Word:wisdom ratio** — Value delivered per word; raised by cutting (Ch 8).

**"Would I say this in person?"** — Pre-publish filter for conversational tone (Ch 7).
