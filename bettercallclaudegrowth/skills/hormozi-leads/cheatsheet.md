# $100M Leads — Cheatsheet (Alex Hormozi)

Goal: get **engaged leads** (people who *show* interest in your stuff). Advertising = making known. Double leads → double business.

## The Core Four (only 4 ways to let people know)
|              | One-to-One (private) | One-to-Many (public) |
|--------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Warm** (know you)   | Warm Outreach   | Post Free Content |
| **Cold** (strangers)  | Cold Outreach   | Paid Ads          |

## Offer vs Lead Magnet (what to advertise)
- **Advertise Core Offer directly** → straight for the sale. **Try this first.**
- **Lead with a Lead Magnet** when offer is expensive / people need to know more first. A Lead Magnet = a complete solution to a *narrow* problem; free/low-cost; reveals the next problem your core offer solves. (Salty-pretzels effect.)

## 3 Lead Magnet Types
1. **Reveal Their Problem** — diagnosis (speed test, posture/termite inspection).
2. **Samples & Trials** — brief full access to core offer (best for recurring solutions).
3. **One Step of a Multi-Step Process** — give one valuable step free, sell the rest.

## Key Numbers & Benchmarks
| Item | Target |
|------|--------|
| **Rule of 100** | 100 primary actions/day × 100 days straight |
| **Warm outreach** | ~100 reach-outs/day (up to 3x each); ~1 customer per 100 |
| **Client-Financed Acquisition** | customer pays > (cost to get + fulfill) within **30 days** → fund infinite scale |
| **LTGP : CAC** | aim **≥ 3 : 1** (lift by raising LTGP or cutting CAC) |
| **Give : Ask cadence** | **Give-Give-Give… until they ask** (over-give, under-ask to grow) |
| **Ad test budget** | spend up to **2× 30-day cash** per new ad before killing |

## Core Four on Steroids — More / Better / New
- **More** — same thing, higher volume (what stops me doing 10x right now?).
- **Better** — find the constraint, test until it frees, then add volume.
- **New** — only after exhausting More+Better. Order: new placement → new platform → new core-four activity.

## The 4 Lead Getters (others do it for you)
| # | Getter | What they do |
|---|--------|--------------|
| 1 | **Customers / Referrals (Word of Mouth)** | buy, then tell others (target ≥25% from referrals) |
| 2 | **Employees** | people inside your business who get leads |
| 3 | **Agencies** | service businesses that get leads / teach you skills |
| 4 | **Affiliates & Partners** | businesses who tell *their* audience about you |

## Roadmap — Order of Operations (Levels 1→7)
1. **Warm Outreach** — first engaged leads / first 5 clients.
2. **+ Post Free Content** (max your personal capacity; paid ads w/ lead magnet).
3. **Hire Employees** to advertise for you.
4. **Fix product → Referrals** (≥25%), then scale ads with a bigger team.
5. **Expand**: new audiences/placements → 2nd method + platform + lead-getter.
6. **Hire killer executives** to run channels without you.
7. (1B+ — TBD)

**Final law:** It's an inputs→outputs game. *You cannot lose if you do not quit.*
