# Cheatsheet — Productize Yourself (Jack Butcher)

## The 3 Steps (non-skippable, in order)
| # | Step | One-liner |
|---|------|-----------|
| 1 | **Put in the reps** | Hone the skill + publish (the unscalable work) |
| 2 | **Discover repeating processes** | Systematize what you do for every client |
| 3 | **Solve problems at scale** | Turn skill + systems into products that sell 24/7 |

## Step 2 — systematize toolkit
- Email templates · Process docs · Intake forms · Niche copy (clients self-select)
- Goal: **"lose your job"** → replace yourself with systems.

## Step 3 — product menu
- Short self-published **book** · **Course** (studio or voiceover-on-slides) · **Templates** · **Software/automation**

## Core principles (memorize)
- **Linear → Exponential**: stop renting time; build assets that earn while you sleep.
- **10,000 iterations, not 10,000 hours.**
- **Get going, then get good.**
- **Proof of work > résumé.**
- **Brand = curiosity × time spent pursuing it** (the Iceberg).
- **Advice is abundant, execution is scarce.**
- **Seek wealth, not money or status** — own equity (Naval).
- **You get rich by giving society what it wants but can't yet get — at scale** (Naval).

## Find your niche / brand — 3 prompts
1. Ask friends: *"What would I be great at writing about?"*
2. Brain-dump interests → circle the 1–3 with most energy.
3. Review sent emails: what do you keep answering?

## Deliberate Practice — 4 ingredients
Specific goal → purposeful reps → target weaknesses → feedback system.

## Publishing routine
Fixed time slot · content calendar · decide topics in advance · **engage with everyone who engages**.

## The decision tree
```
Capped by hours in a day?
        │ yes
        ▼
What am I uniquely good at AND enjoy?  ──► niche to that one thing
        │
        ▼
Have I put in the reps (iterations + proof of work)?
        │ no → keep repping & publishing
        │ yes
        ▼
Have I systematized the repeating work?
        │ no → templatize emails/docs/forms; niche copy
        │ yes
        ▼
Productize: book / course / template / software  ──► sell 24/7
        │
        ▼
Distribute via an audience built over years (Brand Iceberg)
```

## The illusion to reject
"Overnight success" = a decade of invisible reps + consistent publishing + one decision to solve problems that scale. **No shortcuts.**
