# Capitolo 3: GEO Distribution — Visibility Is Built Everywhere AI Reads

## Core Idea
GEO is radically off-site: LLMs read Reddit, YouTube, G2, Capterra, Wikipedia, Medium, LinkedIn — and your mention on these platforms carries as much weight (or more) as content on your own blog.

## Frameworks Introduced
- **Map of sources that LLMs cite** (Semrush 2025, 150k citations): Reddit 40.1%, Wikipedia 26.3%, YouTube 23.5%. But it varies by model: ChatGPT→Wikipedia #1 (~8%); Perplexity→Reddit 46.7%; Gemini→Reddit 0.1%. **The platform to invest in depends on the model that matters most to your target.**
- **YouTube as a quick win**: most-cited domain in Google AI Overviews (29.5%); citation share AIO +414% in 2025, how-to +651%. Cited videos: long-form (94% above 10 min), sweet spot 10-20 min, title = exact commercial query.
- **3× B2B effect (G2/Capterra/Trustpilot)**: domains with active profiles on all three receive 3× more ChatGPT citations. Minimum target: 20-30 reviews, rating 4.3+.
- **Republish with canonical (Medium/LinkedIn)**: publish canonical on your blog → wait 1-2 weeks → republish with `rel=canonical` pointing to the blog. Frequently cited by Perplexity and Copilot.

## Mental Models
- Reddit is a **participation strategy, not an editorial one**: 99% of citations go to discussion threads, not brand profiles. You need an account with real karma + disclosure ("I work at X").
- Digital PR = ask ChatGPT "the best 10 articles reviewing [category]", contact individual writers with data (not the generic editor@).

## Minimum YouTube GEO Playbook
1. One video per BoFu listicle (slideshow + voice-over is enough).
2. Title = commercial query: "Best [category] tools 2026: tested 12, here are the 5 worth your time".
3. Text transcript published on the site → doubles the citation rate.
4. VideoObject schema on the page that embeds it.
5. Rich description + chapters (chapter markers) that reproduce the listicle.

## Anti-patterns
- **Link spam on Reddit** → permanent bans. **Astroturfing** (multiple accounts) → detected quickly. **Discount promos outside dedicated subreddits** → subreddit ban.
- **Client logos in image-only format** → invisible to LLMs (must appear as text).
- **AI-only videos on YouTube**: great for citation share but YouTube demonetizes "AI-only" channels since 2024 — minimum perceived quality is required.

## G2/Capterra Setup (12-16 hours, one-time)
1. Free profiles on G2 + Capterra (sponsorship optional).
2. Complete categories, description, screenshots, integrations.
3. One-shot review campaign targeting happy customers.
4. Update quarterly with features/screenshots.

## Key Takeaways
1. Channel selection follows the target model, not a single universal source ranking.
2. YouTube is the most underrated and fastest-growing channel for AIOs.
3. For B2B SaaS, review-site profiles are in the top-5 ChatGPT sources.
4. The ecosystem is volatile: in September 2025 ChatGPT cut Reddit citations from ~60% to ~10%.
